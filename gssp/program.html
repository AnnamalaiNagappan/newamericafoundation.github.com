Orphan Care Programme

The program was started to provide food baskets, psychological counseling, and to facilitate the exemption from school fees for orphans. It provides poverty relief for destitute persons and orphans.

Cash

Monthly cash benefits (P61) (US$ 8.3) and monthly food rations (equal to P172 = US$ 23,5) to all destitute residents, including those unable to support themselves because of old age, disability, or a chronic health condition; needy children younger than age 18 with a terminally ill parent; or orphans or abandoned children younger than age 18 not covered by the orphan care program.
