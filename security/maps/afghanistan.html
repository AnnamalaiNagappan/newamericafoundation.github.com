Incident Details

Incidents of "green-on-blue" violence were rare in the first few years of the Afghan War, averaging no more than one a year through 2008. With the "surge" of 33,000 U.S. troops in 2009, though, the number of attacks jumped to four. This upward trend continued to a peak of 12 incidents in 2011 -- and there have already been 34 such attacks in 2012.
